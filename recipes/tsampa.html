Tsampa

Description

Tsampa is made from roasted barley. Tsampa is a Tibetan staple food. The most common way to eat tsampa is to mix it by hand with butter tea.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of dried pearled barley
  • butter

Steps

  1. Wash the barley thoroughly with water. Drain off the water.
  2. Spread it out on a flat surface and let it dry.
  3. Once it is mostly dry, you are ready to roast.
  4. Heat sand on high on the stove.
  5. Pour barley on top of the heated sand and roast the barley, shaking it together with the sand in the pan.
  6. Sift out the sand from the roasted barley using a sifter.
  7. Repeat the sifting process 3 to 4 times to properly sift out the sand from the roasted barley.
  8. Grind the clean roasted barley.

If you don't want to go through the process of making Tsampa, try your luck finding some readymade being sold at Tibetan shop or restaurant.
